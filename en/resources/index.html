Academia

  • Iran Data Portal
    Source:
    Iran Data Portal

    The Iran Social Science Data Portal is an English- and Persian-language online portal that features social science data on Iran, including socioeconomic data, electoral data, information on political parties, and translations of selected laws and regulations.

Education

  • Data Gathering for Beginners
    Source:
    Advocacy Assembly

    Are you interested in data journalism or using data to help your advocacy or social development project? Can data help you understand and analyse your challenges better but you don’t know where to start? This course is a gentle introduction to data basics where you can find data and what to do with it.

  • Cleaning and Analysing Data
    Source:
    Advocacy Assembly

    This course will introduce you to the practical skills needed to analyse data using spreadsheets, allowing you to start looking for stories in data if you’re a journalist or analysing your data for advocacy or social development if you’re an activist.

Open Data Organisations

  • Open Knowledge International
    Source:
    Open Knowledge International

    Open Knowledge International is a worldwide non-profit network of people passionate about openness, using advocacy, technology and training to unlock information and enable people to work with it to create and share knowledge.

Open Government

  • Open Data USA
    Source:
    data.gov

    data.gov is the USA's open government platform. It hosts data from government departments, along with analysis and visualisation tools.

  • Open Data UK
    Source:
    data.gov.uk

    data.gov.uk is the UK government's official open data platform. The site hosts datasets from all central government departments and a number of other public sector bodies and local authorities.
